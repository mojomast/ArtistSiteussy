# Cedric Taillon Artist Website Copy

## About the Artist / À propos de l'artiste

### English

**Cédric Taillon** is a visionary Montreal-based contemporary artist whose vibrant work has been transforming the visual arts landscape for over 18 years. Following his formative studies in cartooning in Toronto (2005), he returned to Montreal where he has become an integral force in the city's thriving artistic ecosystem.

Renowned for his masterful command of acrylics and markers on canvas, Taillon creates captivating portraits and dynamic abstract compositions that pulse with the energy and multicultural spirit of Montreal's urban landscape. His artistic evolution has been profoundly shaped by Montreal's street art heritage, having been mentored by the iconic Zïlon (Raymond Pilon), a pioneering figure in Montreal's street art movement whose legacy continues to inspire following his passing in 2023.

Taillon's multifaceted practice encompasses roles as artist-painter, cultural event producer, and former co-director of the influential DÉCOVER magazine, which championed emerging Montreal artists. His profound fascination with human form and live portraiture drives his artistic core, seamlessly transitioning between photorealistic representations and boldly imaginative creations that challenge conventional boundaries.

Throughout his distinguished career, Taillon has graced numerous exhibitions and festivals, including the celebrated Mtl en Arts festival where he has served both as featured artist and esteemed jury member. His dedication to live painting performances has established him as a cherished figure in Montreal's cultural tapestry, democratizing art through immersive, real-time creative experiences that bridge the gap between artist and audience.

Son évolution artistique a été profondément façonnée par l'héritage street art de Montréal, ayant été mentoré par l'iconique Zïlon (Raymond Pilon), figure pionnière du mouvement street art montréalais dont l'héritage continue d'inspirer suite à son décès en 2023.

La pratique multifacette de Taillon englobe les rôles d'artiste-peintre, producteur d'événements culturels, et ancien co-directeur de l'influent magazine DÉCOVER, qui défendait les artistes émergents montréalais. Sa fascination profonde pour la forme humaine et le portrait en direct anime son cœur artistique, transitionnant sans effort entre les représentations photoréalistes et les créations audacieusement imaginatives qui défient les frontières conventionnelles.

Tout au long de sa carrière distinguée, Taillon a honoré de nombreuses expositions et festivals, incluant le célèbre festival Mtl en Arts où il a servi tant comme artiste vedette que membre estimé du jury. Son dévouement aux performances de peinture en direct l'a établi comme une figure chérie de la tapisserie culturelle montréalaise, démocratisant l'art à travers des expériences créatives immersives en temps réel qui comblent l'écart entre artiste et public.
